About Us

Welcome to the home of Appleway Bytes & Bar. Owners Alessia Hoey, Katie Vancleve, and Kim Hicks are a trio of women who lead different lives but share one major thing in common - Grand Circus Bootcamp. We have the fortune of working with each other on our first group project. We immediately selected the website type and started brainstorming. In the spirit of our recent test-driven development lab, which incorporated Pig Latin, our restaurant, Appleway Bytes & Bar, was born.
